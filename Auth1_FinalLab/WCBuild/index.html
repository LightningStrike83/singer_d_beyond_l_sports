USA

The USA is located in between Canada and Mexico. The country itself is made up of 50 states, all of which are represented on the flag by a star. Their most famous landmark is the Statue of Liberty, located in New York. Also in New York is another famous landmark, known as the Empire State Building.

Their football team goes by a couple of different nicknames; The Stars and Stripes or The Yanks. Their current captain is Tyler Adams and presently, their head coach is Gregg Berhalter. They have not won any FIFA World Cups as of yet, but there's always hope for 2026! The closest they have gotten is 8th place in 2002.

France

France is a country located in Europe, just in between Spain, Germany, Belgium, Switzerland, and Italy. Its most famous attraction is the Eiffel Tower, but also home to the Louvre; which also houses the original Mona Lisa painting.

Their football team called called "Les Bleus", translated as "The Blues". Their current captain is Hugo Lloris and their head coach is Didier Deschamps. They have won two FIFA World Cups: One in 1998 and one in 2018.
